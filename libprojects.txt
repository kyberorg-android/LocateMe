Here will be description of library projects our application depends on

1. Android Commons Library by Virtalab (https://github.com/virtalab/android-commons)
2. GeoLib by Virtalab (https://github.com/virtalab/geolib)
3. Google Play Services (Can be obtained as part of Extras in Android SDK)
4. Google Support Library v4 (Can be found in Android SDK)
5. View Pager Indicator by JakeWharton (https://github.com/JakeWharton/Android-ViewPagerIndicator)
